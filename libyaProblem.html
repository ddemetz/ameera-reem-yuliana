What's Happening in Libya?

After overthrowing and killing Mummar Gadaffi, the loathed and depraved leader who forced his rule of Libya, in 2011, Libya has seen yet another civil war since 2014 with another corrupt leader, Khalifa Hafter backed by few countries who want to take over Libyan resources, who wishes to rule over the land. The people, however,say otherwise and have fought for their freedom ever since, while Hafter has yet to back down, killing the people of the beautiful land each day.
